## [0.1.0] — TBD

### Added
- Transparent wrapper around `claude` and `codex` with per-response token accounting.
- Privy browser-loopback login (`login`, `whoami`, `logout`).
- `usage` and `status` commands backed by `api.claudenomics.xyz`.
- Signed receipts persisted locally and auto-submitted to the backend.
